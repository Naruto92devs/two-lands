    WHO ARE WE?

    Who are we and where did we come from? Well, those are the two greatest mysteries in the history of mankind. The duality inherent to the Two Lands ecosystem is personified as a bridge between our physical world and the cosmos of De-Fi, all in an effort to help garner awareness for and fund archaeological expedition & research that may contribute to the completion of our species’ story. In essence, we are like minded individuals with a passion for deciphering and uncovering the true mysteries of our origin, “to help rewrite history, one block at a time”.
